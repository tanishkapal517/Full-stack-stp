Q: How can I book a tour?

A: You can contact us via phone, email, or visit our office. Online booking will be added soon.

Q: Are your packages customizable?

A: Yes, all our packages can be customized as per your preferences.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept UPI, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, and PayPal.

Q: Are flights and meals included in the package?

A: Most packages include accommodation, sightseeing, and transportation. Flights and meals may or may not be included—please check the package details.

Q: What should I carry on a trip?

A: We recommend carrying valid ID proof, comfortable clothing, personal medicines, and travel essentials. A full checklist is provided after booking.

Q: Is travel insurance provided?

A: Travel insurance is not included by default, but can be arranged on request at an additional cost.

Q: . Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

A: Yes, bookings can be canceled or rescheduled as per our cancellation policy. Please read the terms on our “Policies” page.
